ProgressiveMacros Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

ProgressiveMacros is a local-first fitness and nutrition tracking app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy describes how your data is handled.

Data stored on your device

All workout logs, nutrition entries, food goals, and personal data you enter are stored exclusively on your device. This data is never transmitted to or accessible by us.

Food search

When you search for food items, your search query is sent to our server solely to retrieve nutritional information from the FatSecret database. Search queries are not stored on our servers, are not linked to your identity, and are not used for any purpose other than returning search results.

FatSecret's privacy policy is available at: https://platform.fatsecret.com/api/Default.aspx?screen=pp

Data we do not collect

We do not collect your name, email address, location, device identifiers, or any personal information. We do not use analytics, advertising, or crash reporting services.

Third-party services

This app uses the FatSecret Platform API to retrieve food nutritional data. Please refer to FatSecret's privacy policy linked above for information on how they handle data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
